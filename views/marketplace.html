Seoul Dungeon Overflow Raid

All hunters and guilds that participated at the Dungeon Overflow raid that happened last week are eligible to participate at the upcoming bidding of exclusive equipments and deals from Vanguard. The Special Auction event would happen at March 23, 2020 at the 14th floor of Mirae building

Each guild can only send one (1) representative at the bidding.

Some of the invited guilds that we're verified by the company are the following:

- Magic Dragon Clan (Japan)
- Metal Knights (England)
- Magia Clan (Italy)
- Lightning God Clan (S. Korea)

For all the guilds and hunters that participated in the raid, please contact our office: +82 XX XXXX YYYY
Please provide sufficient evidence of your participation in the raid.

Japan Dungeon Wave and Overflow Raid

All hunters and guilds that helped with the evacuation and monster clearing from the recent Dungeon Wave and Overflow that happened at several districts at Japan are eligible to participate at the special sale happening at April 27, 2020.


Schedule


Vanguard's physical store at the reinforced Gangnam district would only operate at Mondays, from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

The physical store are located at the 13th floor of the Mirae Building. The 14th floor is STRICLY OFF-LIMITS and would only be opened during special events such as the Vanguard's Special Auction.
